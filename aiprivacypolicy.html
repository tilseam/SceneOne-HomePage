SceneOne AI Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is the developer's priority.

Introduction

Thank you for using SceneOne AI. This Privacy Policy is maintained by an individual developer and is intended to explain, clearly and concisely, how the developer collects, uses, and protects your information. SceneOne AI is the AI-enhanced feature of the SceneOne production scheduling application, designed to assist film and video creators in producing professional shooting plans efficiently.

Last updated: January 11, 2026

About SceneOne AI

SceneOne AI is integrated into SceneOne's professional planning and scheduling software. The developer's AI capabilities include:

  • AI Storyboard Generation: Assist in quickly producing professional storyboard text to streamline creative workflows.
  • AI Reference Image Generation: Provide visual references to inspire shot design and creative direction.
  • One-Click Generation: Combine the above features into a single-step generation process.

SceneOne AI aims to help you create professional production plans more quickly so you can focus on creative execution.

1. Information Collected by the Developer

1.1 Information You Provide

Registration information: To use SceneOne AI services, you are required to provide a valid email address to register an account. The developer uses your email address as your account identifier for account management, service notifications, and technical support.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Usage data: The developer records logs related to your use of SceneOne AI features, including feature usage, error reports, and performance metrics. These data are used solely for:

  • Error analysis and troubleshooting
  • Performance optimization
  • Improving user experience
  • Providing technical support

AI-generated content: The developer may collect prompts you submit and the generated text and images. Such information is used for troubleshooting, support, and product improvement.

1.3 Local Data

As a local application, SceneOne stores project files and production plans on your device. These files are under your control and will not be uploaded to servers without your explicit notice and consent.

2. Third-Party AI Model Services

SceneOne AI leverages industry-leading AI models to provide intelligent generation features. The developer may use, but is not limited to, the following models:

2.1 Text Generation Models

  • OpenAI
  • Deepseek
  • Anthropic (Claude)
  • Google (Gemini)
  • Qwen

2.2 Image Generation Models

  • Midjourney
  • Google Imagen
  • Qwen VL
  • Leonardo AI
  • Seedream

2.3 Important Notice

When you use SceneOne AI, the content you submit to an AI model (including prompts and generation requests) will be processed by the respective AI service provider and will be subject to that provider's privacy policy. The developer recommends that you review the privacy policies of those third-party services:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Privacy & Gemini: https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13594961?hl=en
  • Deepseek Privacy Policy: https://cdn.deepseek.com/policies/en-US/deepseek-privacy-policy.html
  • Qwen (Alibaba) Privacy Policy: https://www.alibabacloud.com/help/en/legal/latest/alibaba-cloud-international-website-privacy-policy
  • Midjourney Privacy Policy: https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/32083472637453-Privacy-Policy
  • Leonardo AI Privacy Policy: https://leonardo.ai/privacy-policy/
  • Seedream Privacy Policy: https://seedream.pro/privacy

Copyright notice: Content generated by AI models may be subject to the copyright and usage terms of the respective AI service provider. Different providers may apply different rules to generated content.

3. Use and Protection of Information

3.1 Use of Information

The developer uses collected information only for the following legitimate purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide core SceneOne AI functionality, including text and image generation.
  • Account management: To manage your account by email and send service notifications and important updates.
  • Technical support: To assist you in resolving technical issues using recorded information.
  • Error analysis: To analyze software errors and exceptions and to quickly locate and fix issues.
  • Product improvement: To improve product features based on usage data and to optimize model routing.
  • Service enhancement: To understand feature adoption and inform product development.

3.2 Developer Commitments

  • No sale of data: The developer will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • Access controls: The developer does not permit unauthorized third-party access, reading, or use of your personal information.
  • Limited use: Your information will be used only for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.
  • Technical safeguards: Reasonable technical and administrative measures are employed to protect your data.

3.3 Data Security Measures

Although the developer is an individual with limited resources, the following measures are taken to protect your data:

  • Use of encrypted transport protocols (HTTPS) for data in transit
  • Access controls to limit access to user data
  • Regular backups to reduce the risk of data loss
  • Timely application of security patches to mitigate threats

4. Information Sharing

Except as described below, the developer will not share your personal information with third parties:

4.1 AI Service Providers

When you use AI generation features, your prompts and requests may be transmitted to the relevant AI model service provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) in order to fulfill the requested service.

4.2 Legal Requirements

The developer may disclose your information when required to do so by law, including to:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal process or governmental requests
  • Protect the rights, safety, or property of users, the public, or the developer

4.3 With Your Consent

The developer may share information with third parties where you have explicitly authorized such sharing.

5. Data Retention

The developer retains your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described:

  • Account information: Retained for the duration your account is active
  • Usage logs: Typically retained for at least 24 hours for error analysis, troubleshooting, and service optimization
  • AI-generated content: Typically retained for at least 24 hours to allow continued use in intermittent network conditions

When you delete your account or when retention periods expire, the developer will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where retention of such information is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal information the developer holds about you
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: In certain circumstances, you may request deletion of your account and personal data

To exercise these rights, please use the options provided within the application or contact the developer as described below.

7. Local Data Cache

To improve user experience, certain data may be cached locally on your device. Such cached data remain on your device and are not transmitted to the developer's servers; security of cached data is subject to your local device environment and the developer is not responsible for issues arising from the device environment.

You may purchase SceneOne AI services or subscriptions via the Apple App Store or other platforms designated by the developer:

  • You authorize the developer to charge the designated payment method for fees, including renewals
  • Service prices may change; you will be charged at the updated price where applicable
  • Top-up balances will be used according to the specified settlement rules; features listed as non-refundable are not eligible for refunds
  • Payments are generally non-refundable, though the developer may offer refunds in exceptional circumstances

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment processors. The developer does not store full payment card details.

9. Children's Privacy

SceneOne AI is not directed to children under 13 years of age. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe the developer may have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact the developer at service@sceneone.cc and the developer will take steps to delete the information.

By using this service, you represent that:

  • You are at least 13 years old, or have obtained parental or legal guardian consent to use the service
  • You have the legal capacity to understand and comply with this Privacy Policy

Your personal information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. These jurisdictions may have different data protection laws. When cross-border transfers occur:

  • The developer will take appropriate measures to protect your information

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices, technology, or legal requirements. Where material changes occur, the developer will notify you by:

  • Posting a notice within the application
  • Sending an email notification where applicable
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page

Please review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of SceneOne AI constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or other business transfer, your personal information may be transferred as part of the business assets. In such cases:

  • The developer will notify you in advance
  • The developer will require the recipient to honor this Privacy Policy or provide equivalent protections
  • You retain any rights available under applicable law

You may not transfer your rights or obligations under this Privacy Policy without the developer's prior written consent.

13. Contact the Developer

If you have any questions, comments or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact the developer:

Email: service@sceneone.cc

The developer will respond as soon as reasonably practicable, typically within 15 business days. Complex requests may require additional time, and the developer will inform you of any delays.

14. Your Consent

By using SceneOne AI, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the developer's services.

Thank you for trusting SceneOne AI. The developer is committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure, professional service.
